Mediterranean Couscous Salad

Mediterranean couscous salad is a light, refreshing dish made with fluffy couscous, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, olives, and feta cheese, tossed in a zesty lemon-olive oil dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups of dry couscous
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 1 Onion
  • 150g of Raisins
  • 2 large tomatoes tomatoes
  • 1 medium red bellpepper
  • 1 medium yellow bellpepper
  • 200g of feta cheese
  • 1 Lime
  • 2-3 stems of cilantro
  • 30g of Cashew nuts
  • Honey
  • Paprika powder
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place couscous in a bowl and pour boiling water over top. Add salt and pepper. Gently stir, then cover and let sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile cut tomatoes, bell peppers, and feta chesse into small cubes. Add and mix in a separate salad bowl
  4. Finely cut cilantro and add to a bowl for the sauce. Add 3 table spoons of olive oil, 2 tea spoons of lime juice, 4 table spoons of honey, and 2 table spoons of water into the bowl. Season with salt, pepper and paprika powder at your taste.
  5. Crunch the cashew nuts into smaller pieces and hit them up in a pan until slightly brown. Later, add to the vegetables
  6. Mix the sauce with the previously cut vegetables and nuts.
  7. Start adding cooked couscous to the salad while mixing with the rest.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
