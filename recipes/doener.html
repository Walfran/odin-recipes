Döner Kebab

A döner kebab is seasoned lamb, beef, or chicken cooked on a vertical rotisserie, sliced thin, and served in flatbread or on a plate with salad, vegetables, and sauces like garlic or chili. It is a popular street food, especially in Europe.

Ingredients

  • 500g lamb cutlets
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 3 teaspoon of marjoram
  • 2 teaspoon for thyme
  • 3 teaspoon of paprika powder, sweet
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Not covered in this recipe: fries & salad
  2. Gather all ingredients.
  3. Wash the cutlets, dry them, and cut them into strips.
  4. It's best to put the ingredients in a can or bowl so the meat can marinate well.
  5. Grind the cumin in a mortar and pestle before adding it to the rest of the ingredients.
  6. Add the spices and other ingredients and mix everything well. Let it marinate in the fridge overnight, ideally.
  7. Heat a pan over quite high and fry the gyro until crispy all over. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
